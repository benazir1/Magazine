Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidroh Kobi(rebel poet) around the country.Nazrul is a famous poet,musician and philosopher of Bangldesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of that period of the british goverment. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice againt any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the India subcontinent.

Awesome writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism,including religious,caste-based and gender-based, Throughtout his career.Nazrul wrote short stories,novels and eassy but is best know for his songs and poems.He pioneered new music form such as Bengali ghazals.Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records),collectively know as Nazrul Geeti.
